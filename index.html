My favourite Hero's

Fahadh Faasil is a highly acclaimed Indian actor known for his work in Malayalam cinema. Born on August 8, 1982, in Alappuzha, Kerala, Fahadh is recognized for his versatility and compelling performances.

Vijay Sethupathi is a prominent Indian actor known for his work predominantly in Tamil cinema. Born on January 16, 1978, in Rajapalayam, Tamil Nadu, he initially worked as a background actor before rising to fame with his distinctive performances and versatility.

Pawan Kalyan is a prominent Indian actor and politician, primarily known for his work in Telugu cinema. Born on September 2, 1971, in Bapineedu, Andhra Pradesh, he is the younger brother of the renowned actor Chiranjeevi.

Nani, whose full name is Naveen Babu Ghanta, is a popular Indian actor known primarily for his work in Telugu cinema. Born on February 24, 1984, in Hyderabad, Telangana, he has earned a reputation for his versatility and relatable performances
